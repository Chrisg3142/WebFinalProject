At TechBank we offer many different credit cards for someone to apply for, even students that are enrolled in college can apply for a student credit card that comes with many benifits that can help them with their daily life. Some of the benefits are no late fees, 5% cash back, alerts for all usage, and many more features.

We require a minimum of a 650 credit score to be able to be accepted for any of our credit cards. We offer our support to help you reach the required score to be able to get a card from us. For student cards we ask you to come in person to speak about any further steps.

We have secure systems so that if you check you credit score above with our credit checker or if you contact us through our email, you will not have to worry about your info getting leaked. You can always come in to one of our banks and talk with one of our workers to be more secure about your information.
